Chrysp

Packaging / 2017

Green. Gem. Stoned.


Chrysp is a line of cannabis products that encourage you to value your intake by cutting out all the unnecessary decorative graphic elements and treat all the details of each Chrysp products as a part of the branding assets.

Translucent materials are used for packaging to emphasize the transparency of information in a way that is not overdone to create a straightforward yet inviting feel.
Instead of using the iconic marijuana leaf to immediately give away the main ingredient, a subtle green spot is used as a visual representation hinting the main ingredient along with California law required THC warning symbol when needed.
The name Chrysp came from the play of the metaphor used to compare cannabis with : a green gemstone called Chrysoprase. Cannabis is green (mostly). It's a gem. and it gets you stoned. It pronounces the same way as the word “crisp” which is our intention to coordinate with the overall brand.

Video / 2017

Available in App store


This video is capturing a factual archive in motion by using the existing materials that are available for everyone: apps (in the App Store in particular). Sula

Spirit Packaging / 2017

Thai herbal liquor


Sula is a Thai spirit brand that specializes in Yadong, a local Thai liquor infused with medical herbs. It’s believed to give energy and strength and is popular among Thai boxers. Sula captures that aspect and bases its formula off of that resulting in 3 tiers from less potent for everyday boost to a more hardcore degree. Targeting U.S. market, the name Sula which is a polite word for alcohol drinks was chosen partly for an easy pronunciation.
