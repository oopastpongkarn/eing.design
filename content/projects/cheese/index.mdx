## Cheese and whattt?

Cheese and Crack is a typeface based on random sketches in my notebook. The name was inspired by the exhibition where it made its first appearance, [Cheese’n’Crackers](http://cheesencrackers.party/Artists), curated by graphic design students at CCA in Fall 2017.
